Universal Enrichment Service (UES) Submit a large volume of data to search over multiple data sources simultaneously

Submitting a new request

Select the appropriate search type for your data.
Always use the UES template to submit data.
Choose a unique filename for your file.

Results

Contacts, location and immigration history are displayed in your results by default. You can deselect any of these as required. Depending on which RBAC (role-based access control) you are assigned to, you may be able to include intelligence reports and travel history in your results.

Advanced options

You can want to choose more specific settings for your results.

Matching

If desired, you can adjust the Recommended setting for match quality.
By selecting Strict you will receive fewer results, but those matches will be more exact.
By choosing Broad, you will get more results, but these will be less accurate matches.

Data sources

The default list for data sources: BRP (Biometric Residence Permit), CID (Case Information Database), DNB (Dun and Bradstreet), GBD (GB Data), IABS (Immigration and Analysis Biometric System), WPD (Worldpay Data) and PCDP (Person Centric Data Platform). PCDP includes data from Atlas, Pega (EU Settlement Scheme data), CID and CRS (Central Reference System, which records visa information.) Additionally, there are legacy, intelligence and travel data sources.

Again, depending on your RBAC, you may not have access to some specific intelligence of travel datasets (such as SIP, the Single Intelligence Platform).

Progress with your request

A banner will indicate the status of your request (processing or complete). You can download the completed request from a link in the banner or from the table in Your search history. The table shows the status of your current and recent requests.

Usage policy

You must only use Universal Enrichment Service for genuine Home Office business.

Justifications

You must give a justification for using Universal Enrichment Service each time you sign in. This justification is recorded against any searches you do, until you exit Universal Enrichment Service.
