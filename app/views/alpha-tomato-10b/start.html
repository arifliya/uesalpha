Universal Enrichment Service (UES) Submit a large volume of data to search over multiple data sources simultaneously

Before you submit data, you must always specify your settings.

You will need to choose:

  • a search type
  • exact or potential matching
  • details to display in download, such as location, contact information, associations, immigration case history and presence in intelligence products

About UES

Universal Enrichment Service gathers data from multiple data sources, making it quicker and easier to bulk search for information about an entity (such as a person or organisation).

Usage policy

You must only use Universal Enrichment Service for genuine Home Office business.

Justifications

You must give a justification for using Universal Enrichment Service each time you sign in. This justification is recorded against any searches you do, until you exit Universal Enrichment Service. You can see a list of your previous searches and justifications in your search history.
