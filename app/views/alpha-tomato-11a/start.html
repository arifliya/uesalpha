UES (Universal Enrichment Service)

UES gathers data from multiple data sources, making it quicker and easier to bulk search for information about an entity (such as a person, organisation or address).

Settings

You must select your settings before you can submit any data.

You need to choose:

  • a search type
  • the type of matching
  • other information to include in the results, such as location, contact information and associates

Settings can be updated for each submission or you may prefer to use the same settings.

Template

The latest Excel template is available on the New request page.
You must use this template to input and upload your data.

Submission history

After you submit a request, you can monitor its progress on the homepage.

About UES

Users

This service is for intelligence officers, analysts and caseworkers in the Home Office.

Usage policy

You must only use UES for genuine Home Office business.

Justification

Each time you sign in to UES, you must provide a justification for your search.
This justification applies to all the searches you do, until you exit UES.
You can change your justification, using the justification option in the navigation menu.
Your justification is recorded in the Submission history on the homepage.
